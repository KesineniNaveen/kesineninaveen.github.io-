What is Data Science?

Data Science is the process of collecting, cleaning, analyzing, modelling, and deploying data-driven solutions using techniques from statistics, machine learning, and programming.

Statistics for Data Science

  • Mean, Median, Mode
  • Standard Deviation, Variance
  • Probability Theory
  • Hypothesis Testing
  • Correlation & Covariance
import numpy as np
np.mean([1,2,3])
numpy.std([10,20,30])
